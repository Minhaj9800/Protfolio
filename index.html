My name is Minhajur Rahman, and I am a Solution Analyst at IT Systems & Services, University of Prince Edward Island (UPEI). Previously, I worked as a Software Developer at Maximus Canada, a Business Intelligence Reporting Analyst for the Government of Canada, and a Machine Learning Developer at Thinking Big Inc.

I hold a First Class Honours Degree in Computer Science from UPEI, where I conducted research in Cloud Computing, with a focus on Dew Computing as a post-cloud computing approach, along with Health Information Systems, Big Data, Predictive Analytics, and Machine Learning. I also worked as a Teaching Assistant and Lab Assistant, mentoring students in Computer Science courses.

My research interests include Machine Learning, Big Data, Predictive Analytics, Health Information Systems, and Cloud Computing. I am passionate about using AI and data-driven solutions to improve outcomes in Healthcare and Higher Education, enhancing decision-making and operational efficiency.
